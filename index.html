Our mission is to provide our customers with all their outdoor needs.

Sunset Chasers is an outdoors company providing consumers with camping, hiking, and hunting equipment, as well as clothing and accessories. Sunset Chasers is partnered with CalTIP, the Wildlife Conservation Society, and other environmental groups in order to ensure environmental sustainability is at the top of the company’s priorities.
